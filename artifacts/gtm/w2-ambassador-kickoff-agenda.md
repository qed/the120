## The 45 minutes

**0:00–0:05 · Welcome + why you're here** *(Peter)*
- "You're the first. Before The 120 is public, I picked a few grade 7/8 kids to help build it from the inside — you're the **Founding Ambassadors**."
- Quick round: each kid says their name + their group (athlete / founder / maker / scholar / giver).

**0:05–0:12 · What The 120 is (fast, kid energy)** *(Peter)*
- 120 kids, five groups, two cohorts. Everyone builds a real business and gets solid at math. 20 weekend workshops across the year, each stretch ending on a **Demo Day** where you present on stage — the first is **Nov 7**.
- Keep it punchy; they mostly know this. Point is the shared language.

**0:12–0:22 · What a Founding Ambassador actually does** *(Peter)*
Four things, all opt-in, zero pressure:
1. **Challenge friends to the Gauntlet** with your code.
2. **Bring one friend's family** to an info session or the August meetup.
3. *(If you want)* give a **2-minute "why I'm in"** at a session — kid voice sells better than any adult.
4. **Show off the Tin Can + Address Book** — it's a genuinely cool object; let people see it.
- Frame: "You're the spark, not a salesperson. One real friend beats a hundred posts."

**0:22–0:30 · The payoff — recognition** *(say this part clearly, it's for the parents too)*
- **Founding Ambassador** title, named in the Address Book.
- **Badge + first pick of demo slot at the Nov 7 Demo Day.**
- Gauntlet leaderboard flair + early access to new levels.
- **No money changes hands, ever. No quotas. Opt out anytime. Everything runs with a parent's consent, and we only ever email families who sign up themselves.**

**0:30–0:38 · The Gauntlet + your code**
- Quick live demo of the Gauntlet (screen-share) — let them feel it.
- "Here's your code: `AMB-[FIRSTNAME]`. When a friend joins from your share, that's on you."
- This week: share the **link + the one-pager**. (Share card coming soon.)

**0:38–0:43 · Q&A** — kids first, then parents.

**0:43–0:45 · The one ask + next steps**
- Each kid picks **one** thing to do this week (challenge 3 friends to the Gauntlet, *or* invite one family). Say it out loud — spoken commitments stick.
- "I'll send your code + the kit right after this, and I'll shout out the first wins at our next check-in."
