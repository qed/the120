**Purpose:** Side-by-side reference showing how the two GT websites differ, since GT Toronto's site should follow **gt.school**, not anywhere.gt.school.
**Snapshot date:** July 3, 2026

## 4. Content & Messaging Differences

**Who it speaks to.** gt.school speaks to a local parent deciding on a school — it sells a place, a community, and a daily life ("A Day in the Life," tours, events, campus rooms). anywhere.gt.school speaks to a parent anywhere in Texas/US evaluating a program — it sells a system, outcomes, and a deal (TEFA-funded, $0 cost).

**Proof style.** Both use the same core stats (1400+ SAT, AP 5s, 3X velocity, top percentiles). gt.school wraps them in narrative and philosophy ("joyful intensity," motivation-is-the-bottleneck). anywhere.gt.school itemizes them into feature cards with explicit parent payoffs ("What parents see: …").

**Events vs. funnel.** gt.school has a living Key Dates section (camps, showcases, coffee chats) — signals a real community you can visit. anywhere.gt.school has no events except the four intensive weekends; every path leads to Begin Application.

**Pricing.** gt.school never states tuition on the homepage; admissions is relationship-first (tour → apply), with scholarships as the hook. anywhere.gt.school leads with the TEFA voucher economics — the price is the pitch.

**Classes framing.** Same underlying catalog, different presentation: gt.school shows electives as part of a school day (icon grid, "mornings for acceleration, afternoons for application"); anywhere.gt.school organizes them as three product tracks in tabs (Science/Humanities/Competition, numbered 01–05).

**Design language.** gt.school: light, photographic, campus-warm, blue accents, real photography carries the page. anywhere.gt.school: dark, technical, blueprint motifs, orange accent, cards and diagrams carry the page.

**Trust signals.** gt.school: Cognia badge, physical address, phone, tour invitation, named locations roadmap. anywhere.gt.school: press logos, TEFA approval, accreditation candidacy disclosure, refund policy — the trust kit of an online purchase.

## 5. What GT Toronto Takes From Each

From **gt.school** (primary): overall look and feel, "School Reimagined" hero energy, one-page-with-anchors architecture + About layer, Key Dates/events section, community-and-belonging narrative, three promises, photo-forward design, locations roadmap pattern ("Toronto campus — coming"), dual CTA pattern (Book a Call replacing Book a Tour, + Apply Now).

From **anywhere.gt.school** (program facts only): the actual product being delivered (2-hour TimeBack core, advisors, cohorts, class catalog), intensives page structure and dates, lead-capture form pattern (minus income field), FAQ scaffolding, press strip.

Deliberately excluded from both: all TEFA/voucher content, US-only compliance language, Georgetown campus claims.
