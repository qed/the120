Toronto campus — coming

The tribe gathers in Toronto

A dedicated campus for the 120 is planned for Toronto — purpose-built for gifted learners, following the blueprint of GT School's Georgetown hub. Until then, intensives run at a Toronto venue shared with member families.

A drive, not a flight, for most Ontario families. We recommend attending at least one Intensive per year to fully integrate into the physical tribe.

48-hour deep execution

Not an event. A sprint.

We don't host field trips. Students execute 48-hour deep sprints producing real work for real audiences.

DAY 1
Synthesis & prototyping
Students arrive with the foundations they built virtually and move directly into physical builds, intensive debate drilling, or adaptive mastery checks alongside their Academic Advisors.
DAY 2
The Capstone Arena
Everything culminates in a live showcase: 120 year-long projects, demoed on stage. Every member presents and defends their quarter's milestone — from a robotics build to a 2,000-word thesis — in front of peers, parents, and external judges.
