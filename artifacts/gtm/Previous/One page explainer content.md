### THE 120
#### Build your network. Build what you love into a real business. Get solid with the math to run it.

**Athletes build their NIL. Founders, a company. Makers, a studio. Scholars, a research lab. Givers, a cause.** At The 120, every kid turns what they love into the real thing behind it — with the network to build it alongside, and the math to run the numbers.

**120 kids. Five groups. Ages 8–17. Toronto. Founding cohort, Fall 2026.**

Toronto's most motivated and engaged kids — athletes, founders, makers, scholars, givers — one network, capped at 120 seats, on purpose.

> **What you'll actually do this year:** 20 weekend workshops to build a real business, and get solid with math to be able to run the numbers.

---

### The five groups
- **The Athletes** — train seriously, compete seriously, think like a pro.
- **The Founders** — start something real: customers, revenue, lessons learned.
- **The Makers** — art, film, music, invention. A real body of work, shipped.
- **The Scholars** — accelerated, mastery-based academics with no ceiling.
- **The Givers** — lead real service. Projects that change a corner of the city.

---

### Membership is three things
1. **A Tin Can + the Address Book.** A screen-free "bat phone" to 119 other kids building interesting lives, across all five groups.
2. **Build your business.** Across 20 weekend workshops this year, your child builds an actual venture in their domain: Athletes build an NIL business. Founders build a startup. Makers build art, theatre or music shows. Scholars and Givers build research or community service organizations.
3. **Get solid with math.** Mastery-based math acceleration, no ceiling — the numbers sense to actually run what they build. Members routinely learn at ~3× the pace of a traditional classroom.

### How it works
1. **Create an account and build the dossier** — your child's group, interests, and a pitch for what they'll build, in their own words.
2. **Book a call** — we review the dossier together and qualify the fit, group by group.
3. **Join the group** — a seat, a Tin Can, a mentor, and a real business to build across the year.

**Admission first.** Tuition applies only after your child qualifies: dossier review, then the qualifying assessment. No payment until a seat is offered.
