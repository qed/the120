## 2. Project Overview

GT Toronto sells the GT program — accelerated, mastery-based education for gifted K–8 students — to Ontario/GTA families. The delivered product today is the GT Anywhere program (virtual core + advisors + cohorts) with the four quarterly in-person intensives held in Toronto instead of Austin. A dedicated Toronto campus is planned.

**Critical design direction:** The site should look and feel like **gt.school**, not anywhere.gt.school. gt.school is the flagship campus-school brand — "School Reimagined," community, classes, key dates, campus energy. GT Toronto borrows that identity with a **hybrid story**: the program is virtual + Toronto intensives now, campus later. "Book a Tour" becomes **"Book a Call."**

The site's job: convince parents of gifted K–8 children in the GTA to begin an application for Fall 2026.

### Positioning statement

For GTA parents of gifted K–8 kids who are bored and under-challenged in school, GT Toronto is GT School brought to Canada: top 1% academics delivered at 3x learning velocity in 2 focused hours a day, plus life skills workshops and a daily cohort of true intellectual peers — with quarterly in-person intensives right here in Toronto, and a Toronto campus on the way.

## 4. Audience

Primary: Ontario/GTA parents of high-aptitude children in grades K–8 — kids in the top 1–2% who ask for more academics. These parents currently weigh gifted-stream public programs, $25k–$40k GTA private schools, or DIY homeschooling. They want intellectual acceleration AND belonging for their child.

Secondary: existing Ontario homeschooling families seeking a rigorous, structured, measured academic core.

## 5. What GT Toronto Is / Is Not

**Is:**
- The GT program in Canada: 2-hour AI-adaptive mornings (TimeBack), applied-academics afternoons, PhD-level Academic Advisors, daily 12–15 student virtual cohorts
- Life skills through rigorous academic workshops: teamwork, leadership, public speaking, storytelling, financial literacy, entrepreneurship, relationships, socialization, grit, hard work
- Four quarterly 48-hour in-person intensives in Toronto (not Austin)
- Selective: top 1–2%, qualifying assessment required — same bar as GT
- A complete academic core for homeschooling families, structured and advisor-led with quarterly trajectory reports
- On a path to a physical Toronto campus

**Is NOT (site must actively counter these):**
- NOT a tutoring service — it is the complete academic program, not supplemental help
- NOT screen-only isolation — counter the "virtual school = lonely kid on a laptop" fear with cohorts, advisors, and Toronto intensives
- NOT for remediation — built for kids who ask for more, not kids who are struggling
- NOT unstructured homeschooling — structured, measured, advisor-led, with real accountability
- NOT the general Alpha program — that's Alpha Anywhere Canada (alphaanywhere.ca); GT Toronto is exclusively for gifted/high-aptitude students

Keep GT's signature vocabulary: "intellectual tribe," "joyful intensity," "remove the ceiling," "mastery before progression," "3x learning velocity," "kids who finally 'get it,'" "School Reimagined."
