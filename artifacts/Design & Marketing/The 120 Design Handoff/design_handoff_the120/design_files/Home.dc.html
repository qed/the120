HOW IT WORKS

Joining the 120

Book a call and join the group. That's the heart of it. Each group qualifies its members its own way; the Scholars' assessment is run by GT.

STEP 01 Create an account and build the dossier. Your child's profile for the 120: their group, their interests, a project pitch in their own words.
STEP 02 Book a call. We review the dossier together and qualify the fit, group by group.
STEP 03 Join the group. A seat, a Tin Can with the Address Book inside, a mentor, and a year-long project that ships.
