4. **Leaderboard structure** — Three prize pools (one per band), per #1 above.
5. **Migration timing (B1/B2)** — Ethan and Dev to ask Peter for help with databases and other parts when needed.
6. **Tournament entry & ranking — account-to-rank (D1, 2026-07-17)** — entrants get on the board by creating the existing **full account** (the entry modal drives the real `AccountModal` admissions signup), so **every entrant is a full admissions lead — the account *is* the lead capture** — and scores stay account-bound with a real identity for B1 to enforce against. **No separate entries-keyed scoring path.** Accepted trade-off: full-funnel friction suppresses raw entry count, so the entry-flow UX must earn every signup (see B6). Supersedes the earlier "ranking deferred to a B1-joined design" note in `roadmap.md`.
7. **Grade 9–12 ships for real (D2, 2026-07-17)** — The Gauntlet is **Grade 3–12 Fast Math**. Ship a real Grade 9–12 content band + a 9–12 leaderboard by Aug 3 (see B5). The 9–12 content is already authored in `gauntletcontent.md`; the engine build is what's outstanding. **Non-negotiable — no asterisk, no 7–8-content fallback.**
8. **Tournament scoring = difficulty-weighted mastery (2026-07-17, after a 7-persona plan review + scoring-model pass)** — entrants rank by **distinct facts mastered during the window, weighted by content band** (a g912 fact worth more than a g34), *not* cumulative XP. Chosen because mastery is **re-demonstrable** (podium winners re-answer a sample of mastered facts live = real cash verification — a cumulative 21-day total can't be re-run), one-time-per-fact (grind-proof), and rewards learning over screen-time. Reuses the M5 mastery model + the A4 "My Facts" heatmap (B1/B5/B6 now validate/aggregate *mastery events*). **Full go-live plan:** `docs/plans/2026-07-17-001-feat-gauntlet-tournament-golive-spine-plan.md` (B5 content + B6 account-to-rank linkage + B1 mastery-event integrity; phased for the W2 soft launch, hardened by Aug 3).
